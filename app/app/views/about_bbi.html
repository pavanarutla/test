About BBI

Billboards India, since its inception in 2017 has taken huge strides in revolutionizing the advertising industry in India. Our activities span Out of Home Advertising (OOH) including Billboards, Unipoles, Bus Shelter etc., to Digital Mediums in Transit like India's first Digital Screens inside Hyderabad Metro Trains. Billboards India is the official partner of L&T Hyderabad Metro for this one-of-a-kind digital medium and boasts clientele across Hyderabad.

As Billboards India sets sights on even more ambitious goals, we remain inspired and guided by the story and philosophy of our CEO,
Mr. Chanikya Varma who followed his dream to change India's advertising climate using technology. An all-around Application for easy management of OOH campaigns and technologically ahead-of-the-curve Metro Screen mediums make sure that Billboards India fully automates the process and gives control to the client as it should be.

Our ultimate aim has always been – and will always be – to touch the lives of people in a positive way.

Goal:

Our first and foremost goal is stellar-customer experience and providing our clients to manage their campaigns at the touch of a button i.e. making sure the client is in control as it should be with the help of cutting-edge technology.

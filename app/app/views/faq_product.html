Frequently Asked Questions

How can Billboards India help me?

  • Billboards India is an out-house advertising company which basically promotes the customers’ campaign across the Hyderabad city. The major mode of advertisements would be through the TV screens on the Metro.

Why should we partner with Billboards India?

  • Billboards India is the only company that has partnered with the L&T Metro Hyderabad for digital screens inside the metro trains.

On how many screens would an advertisement be played?

  • There are about 12 screens in a metro car, and there are totally about 35 trains traversing throughout the city.

Would the advertisements be played simultaneously on every screen in a train?

  • Yes, the advertisement would indeed be played simultaneously on every screen in a metro train.

Would the content for the advertisement campaign be curated by the company?

  • Billboards India does create content for the respective campaigns when desired by the customer or the pre tailored content shall be promoted. Depends on the customers’ expectations and wishes.

What are the other modes of advertising other than metro screens?

  • Billboards India also promotes through various OOH media using its robust application to track and save campaigns on the fly.

How effective is advertising through the metro screens?

  • Hyderabad metro is being used by about 3 lakh commuters per day.
  • Furthermore to keep the people engaged, Billboards India runs “Metro-Maza”, an entertainment stream collected from various talented artists across the world which increases engagement.

How to book slots for advertisement in Billboards India?

  • All our branding inventory can be booked exclusively from our All-in-one Application where you can create, track, edit and save campaigns on the fly.

How many hours of running time does each train have?

  • Each Metro train has a running time of 16 hours per day.

What are Spots?

  • Each 30 seconds of run time per screen per day per train is a spot. Each commercial occupies 576 spots per day per train.

What is the geographical reach of the Metro?

  • Metro Spans 2 corridors namely:
    1. Miyapur – LB Nagar
    2. Nagole – Hi-Tech City
  • With the Metro covering the vast part of the city with dynamic audience, this medium easily has a huge geographical reach in Hyderabad.
